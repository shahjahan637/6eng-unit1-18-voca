১. প্রতিটি মূল শব্দ দিয়ে সহজ বাক্য (Examples)

১. Abandoned: There is an abandoned house at the end of the street.
২. Activities: We take part in various school activities.
৩. Against: He was leaning against the wall.
৪. Alluring: The beauty of the hills is very alluring.
৫. Amazing: The sunset was an amazing sight.
৬. Attraction: Birisiri is a great tourist attraction.
৭. Bank: They sat on the bank of the river Someshwari.
৮. Ceramic: Bijoypur is famous for its ceramic soil.
৯. Charming: The scenery of the countryside is very charming.
১০. Coal: There is a coal mine in this region.
১১. Countryside: I love to spend my holidays in the countryside.
১২. Crystal clear: The water of the lake is crystal clear.
১৩. Cultural: We should respect our cultural heritage.
১৪. Culture: Bangladesh has a rich and diverse culture.
১৫. Destination: Susang Durgapur is our next travel destination.

... এবং আরও অনেক বাক্য নিচে টেবিলে অন্তর্ভুক্ত করা হয়েছে।
